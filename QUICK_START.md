# ⚡ Quick Start Guide - 5 Minutes to Mastery

## 🎯 What This App Does

**Problem:** Clients feel ignored, follow-ups are missed, complaints get lost, staff is overwhelmed.

**Solution:** AI-powered CRM that automatically reminds you to follow up, tracks every complaint, and suggests what to do next.

---

## 🚀 Start Using It Now

### Step 1: Open the Dashboard
```
http://localhost:3000
```

You'll see:
- **5 Key Metrics** at the top (Total Clients, Healthy, At Risk, Follow-Ups, Complaints)
- **Client Health Dashboard** showing Green/Yellow/Red status
- **Quick Actions** buttons on the right

---

## 📋 The 5 Main Actions

### 1️⃣ Add New Client
**When:** You get a new customer
**How:** Click "Add New Client" → Enter name, email, phone, company
**Result:** Client appears in dashboard with Green status

### 2️⃣ Log Interaction
**When:** You talk to a client (call, email, meeting, etc.)
**How:** Click "Log Interaction" → Select type → Add notes → Choose sentiment
**Result:** Interaction saved, client health updated automatically

### 3️⃣ Schedule Follow-Up
**When:** You need to remind yourself to check on a client
**How:** Click "Schedule Follow-Up" → Pick date/time → Choose method (WhatsApp/SMS/Email)
**Result:** Reminder scheduled, staff gets notified on that date

### 4️⃣ Report Complaint
**When:** A client has an issue
**How:** Click "Report Complaint" → Add title, description, category, priority
**Result:** Complaint tracked, automatic reminders until resolved

### 5️⃣ View AI Suggestions
**When:** You want to know what to do next
**How:** Click "View AI Suggestions"
**Result:** See at-risk clients, recommended follow-ups, churn predictions

## 🔑 Key Concepts

### Health Status
- **Green:** Client is happy, engaged, recent interaction
- **Yellow:** Client is inactive, needs attention soon
- **Red:** Client is at risk, needs immediate action

### Interaction Types
- **Call:** Phone conversation
- **Email:** Email communication
- **SMS:** Text message
- **WhatsApp:** WhatsApp message
- **Meeting:** In-person or video meeting
- **Note:** Internal note
- **Feedback:** Customer feedback

### Sentiment
- **Positive:** Client is happy, satisfied
- **Neutral:** Normal conversation
- **Negative:** Client is unhappy, frustrated

### Complaint Priority
- **Low:** Can wait, not urgent
- **Medium:** Should be addressed soon
- **High:** Needs attention this week
- **Critical:** Needs immediate action

### Follow-Up Status
- **Pending:** Scheduled but not yet sent
- **Sent:** Reminder sent to staff
- **Completed:** Follow-up done
- **Skipped:** Skipped for a reason

## 📊 Metrics Explained

### Client Health Score (0-100)
- Based on: Interaction frequency, sentiment, complaint resolution
- 0-30: Critical (Red)
- 31-70: At Risk (Yellow)
- 71-100: Healthy (Green)

### Churn Risk (0-100)
- Based on: Days since last interaction, open complaints, negative sentiment
- 0-30: Low risk
- 31-70: Medium risk
- 71-100: High risk (act now!)

## 🚀 Pro Tips

### Tip 1: Log Interactions Immediately
Don't wait until end of day. Log interactions right after they happen so the AI has fresh data.

### Tip 2: Use Sentiment Correctly
- Positive = Client is happy
- Neutral = Normal conversation
- Negative = Client is upset

This helps the AI identify at-risk clients.

### Tip 3: Set Priorities on Complaints
- Critical = Respond today
- High = Respond this week
- Medium = Respond next week
- Low = Can wait

### Tip 4: Review AI Insights Daily
The AI learns from your interactions. The more data you give it, the better its suggestions.

### Tip 5: Use Follow-Up Templates
Create message templates for common follow-ups. Save time and stay consistent.

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: What if I forget to log an interaction?**
A: Log it later. The date field lets you backdate interactions.

**Q: Can I change a client's status manually?**
A: No, status is automatic based on interactions and complaints. This keeps it objective.

**Q: What if a complaint is resolved?**
A: Mark it as "Resolved" in the Complaints tab. System stops sending reminders.

**Q: How often does AI update suggestions?**
A: After every interaction or complaint. Check daily for fresh insights.

**Q: Can I export data?**
A: Currently view in dashboard. Export feature coming soon.

**Q: What if I have multiple team members?**
A: Assign follow-ups and complaints to specific team members.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes. All data stored in secure PostgreSQL database. No data leaves your server.

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Dashboard shows 0 clients
- You haven't added any yet. Click "Add New Client" to start.

### AI Insights are empty
- You need interactions first. Log some interactions, then check insights.

### Follow-up reminder didn't send
- Check the date/time. Make sure it's in the future.

### Complaint status won't change
- Click on the complaint to edit it. Change status and save.

### Can't find a client
- Use browser search (Ctrl+F) to find them on the page.
